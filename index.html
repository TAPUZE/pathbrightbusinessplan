PathBright Learning is a mobile-first educational technology company engineered to become the definitive leader in the U.S. high school equivalency market.

Company Overview

We are not just a test-prep tool; we are a life-changing platform that addresses the acute, unmet needs of the ~450,000 individuals who actively seek a GED credential annually.

Our Mission

To provide a mobile-first, data-driven preparation platform that builds not just knowledge, but the confidence and cognitive resilience adult learners need to succeed.

The Problem

As traditional adult education declines, a large, digitally-native learner population is left without effective, modern support, struggling with motivation and one-size-fits-all tools.

Our Vision

To expand from the market leader in GED prep into a full-stack credentialing platform that can award diplomas and vocational certifications directly.

The Mandate: A Market in Structural Transition

This opportunity is defined by an irreversible shift from analog to digital. PathBright is not creating a new market; we are providing a superior solution to a large, existing market being abandoned by its legacy providers.

Total Addressable Market (TAM)

>39 million U.S. adults without a high school credential.

Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)

~450,000 motivated, needs-driven individuals actively preparing for the GED annually.

Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)

Initial 3-5 year target of 25,000 users, representing a $3M ARR opportunity.

The Solution: An Intelligent Learning Ecosystem

Our platform is engineered to address the specific cognitive and psychological barriers that cause adult learners to fail, powered by a defensible technological advantage.

The Defensible Moat

An exclusive U.S. licensing agreement with a leading international EdTech research firm provides a scientifically-validated personalization engine. This partnership grants us:

  • A Unique Technical Advantage that cannot be easily replicated.
  • Reduced R&D Risk and accelerated time-to-market.
  • Instant Pedagogical Credibility with learners and institutions.

The PathBright Method

A continuous, adaptive loop designed to ensure mastery and build confidence:

  • 1. Granular Diagnostics: Pinpointing not just what a user doesn't know, but *why*.
  • 2. Multi-Modal Delivery: Providing multiple pathways to mastery (video, game, podcast).
  • 3. Intelligent Remediation: Generating custom paths to overcome specific hurdles.
  • 4. Cognitive Flexibility: Creating more effective, resilient learners.

Market Validation: Early Signals of Demand

We have mitigated product-market fit risk through deep, qualitative research into our target user base, confirming an acute need for our solution.

Pain Point 1: Math Anxiety

Analysis of Reddit's r/GED forum reveals profound difficulty with the math section and frustration with existing materials. This validates our need for granular diagnostics and "test-based" micro-lessons.

Pain Point 2: Motivation

Learners openly discuss struggles with procrastination and self-confidence. This validates our integration of proven gamification mechanics (streaks, points) to foster intrinsic motivation.

Institutional Interest (LOIs)

We are actively pursuing non-binding Letters of Intent from a target list of 15 community colleges and workforce boards to provide tangible, third-party validation of our B2B model before fundraising.
